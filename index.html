Start Your Journey: A Simple Guide to Using Yukashi

Getting started is easy, and we’re here to guide you every step of the way. Whether you’re looking to calculate your age, track the time between two significant dates, or reflect on the moments that matter most, Yukashi makes it simple.

1.To Calculate Age:
To calculate your age, simply enter your birthdate in the input field, select the current date, and click Calculate. Yukashi will show you your age in years, months, and days.

2.To Measure Time Between Two Dates:
If you want to measure the time between two dates, just enter both dates in the input fields and hit Calculate. Yukashi will display the exact time passed between them in years, months, and days.

3.To Clear Your Data:
if you want to clear everything, simply click the Reset icon to remove all the data and start fresh.
